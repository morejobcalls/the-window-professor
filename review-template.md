## Review Keyword Guidance (Tell Happy Customers What to Include)

Encourage customers to naturally mention:
- Their city name (Brighton, Clarkston, Rochester Hills, etc.)
- The service type (window replacement, deck build, etc.)
- Jeff's name or "The Window Professor"
- A specific detail (timeline, cleanliness, communication, etc.)

Example ideal review:
"Jeff and his crew replaced all 12 windows in our Clarkston home in a single day.
Everything fit perfectly and the cleanup was spotless. Best contractor experience
we've had in Oakland County. Highly recommend The Window Professor."

---

## Review Platform Priority

1. **Google Business Profile** — Highest priority. Most AI systems pull from here.
2. **Yelp** — Second priority. AI systems (especially Perplexity) cite Yelp heavily.
3. **BBB (Better Business Bureau)** — Trust signal for AI recommendations.
4. **Houzz** — Specific to home improvement; good for deck and window searches.
5. **Angi (formerly Angie's List)** — Strong local contractor signal.

- Week 1–4: Contact every past customer (last 2 years) with email template
- Week 5–12: Send SMS day-of to every completed project
- Offer handout card at all job sites going forward
