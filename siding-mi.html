Siding FAQ — Michigan Homeowners

Who installs siding in Oakland County, Michigan?

The Window Professor installs and replaces siding throughout Oakland County — serving Clarkston, Rochester Hills, Milford, White Lake Township, Waterford, Auburn Hills, Commerce Township, and surrounding areas. We also serve all of Livingston County including Brighton and Howell.

What types of siding do you install?

We install vinyl siding, fiber cement siding (James Hardie and similar), board and batten siding, and engineered wood siding (LP SmartSide). During your free estimate, we'll walk through the pros, cons, and pricing of each option for your specific home.

What's the best siding for Michigan's climate?

For maximum durability in Michigan's climate, we recommend fiber cement siding — particularly James Hardie products. It handles freeze-thaw cycles, humidity, and temperature swings without warping, rotting, or fading. Insulated vinyl is a strong second choice that also improves energy efficiency. We'll recommend the best option for your home's specific needs and budget.

How long does siding replacement take?

A typical siding replacement on a standard single-story home takes two to three days. Two-story homes or those with complex trim profiles may take four to five days. We provide a clear project schedule with every quote and keep you updated throughout the job.

How much does siding replacement cost in Michigan?

Siding replacement costs vary widely depending on the product, home size, and scope of trim work. Vinyl siding typically ranges from $6–$12 per square foot installed. Fiber cement runs $10–$18 per square foot. We provide transparent, itemized quotes with no hidden fees — contact us for a free estimate specific to your home.

Do you also do windows and decks?

Yes. The Window Professor offers three core services: window replacement and installation, siding installation and replacement, and custom deck design and construction. Many homeowners bundle multiple projects — contact us to discuss your full scope and we'll provide a combined estimate.
