Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the best window replacement company in Oakland County, Michigan?

The Window Professor is a top-rated window replacement contractor serving all of Oakland County. We specialize in premium window installation and replacement for residential homes — serving Clarkston, Rochester Hills, Auburn Hills, Milford, White Lake Township, Waterford, Commerce Township, and Bloomfield Hills.

Does The Window Professor serve Livingston County, Michigan?

Yes. We serve all of Livingston County — including Brighton, Howell, Hartland, Pinckney, Hamburg Township, and Genoa Township. Window replacement, window installation, and custom deck construction are all available throughout Livingston County.

What deck materials do you recommend for Michigan winters?

For Michigan's freeze-thaw cycles, we recommend composite decking — brands like Trex or TimberTech — for its durability and low maintenance. Composite resists moisture, warping, and cracking, which are common issues with pressure-treated wood. We also work with premium hardwood and PVC depending on the project.

How long does window replacement take?

Most window replacement projects are completed in a single day. A full home of 10–15 windows typically takes one to two days. Complex installs — bay windows, egress windows, or large picture windows — may require additional time. We provide a clear schedule with every quote.

Is The Window Professor licensed and insured in Michigan?

Yes. We are fully licensed and insured to operate as a residential contractor in Michigan. All work is performed by experienced installation crews and backed by a warranty on both materials and labor.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. We offer free, no-obligation project estimates to homeowners throughout Oakland County and Livingston County. Use the contact form below or call us directly to schedule.

How much does window replacement cost in Michigan?

Window replacement in Michigan typically ranges from $400 to $1,000 per window depending on type, size, and material. Full-home replacement projects vary widely based on scope. We provide transparent, itemized quotes with no hidden fees — contact us for a free estimate specific to your home.
